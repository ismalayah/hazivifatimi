Denis Kessler death: How did Denis Kessler die?

Publish date: 2024-06-08

Denis Kessler’s death: How did Denis Kessler die? – Denis Kessler, the industry titan and chairman of French reinsurance powerhouse Scor died on June 9, 2023, at the age of 71. The circumstances behind his demise have yet to be revealed.

He was the chairman of the board of directors and chief executive officer of SCOR SE at the time of his death, a position he had held since 2002.

Kessler was generally praised in the industry for steering Scor from the brink of bankruptcy in 2002 to one of Europe’s largest reinsurers – companies that provide coverage to primary insurers.

Prior to his role at SCOR, he was the Vice-Chairman of MEDEF and the CEO of AXA.

Kessler served on the boards of BNP Paribas, Bolloré, Dassault Aviation, Invesco, and Fonds Strategic d’Investment.

He was a member of the Yam Invest N.V. Supervisory Board and the President of Le Siècle from 2007 to 2010.

Kessler graduated from HEC Paris and received a Ph.D. in Economics from the University of Paris.
