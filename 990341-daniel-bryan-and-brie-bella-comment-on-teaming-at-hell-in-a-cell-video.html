Daniel Bryan And Brie Bella Comment On Teaming At Hell In A Cell (Video)

Publish date: 2024-09-05

WWE posted a new video with Brie Bella and Daniel Bryan talking about Brie’s return to the ring at Hell In A Cell. This match will mark Brie’s first tag team match (and non battle royal match) since competing in a ten-woman tag team match at Wrestlemania 32.

Brie and Daniel talked about the opening of tonight’s show, with Brie saying she really wanted to punch Maryse, not Miz. Bryan says there will be plenty of face punching at Hell In A Cell, and Brie says Maryse should be very scared after seeing what she did to her husband.

Brie comments on how she’s preparing differently for this match than her last WWE appearance in the Women’s Royal Rumble match:

“My husband is helping me with my punches, because that’s what we love to do. I think now I’m just looking at it now as when you’re tagging with someone—obviously we’re married—and we know each other works, but how we can blend together in the ring.”

Bryan then added that they are going to be throwing headbutts and punches, and said the advantage they have is that they are not cowards. Brie added that Bryan is excited to get back in the ring after years off, and she is his backbone and there to support him.
