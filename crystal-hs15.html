Crystal (Japanese: ミサヲ Misao) is a character of the day who appeared in Putting the Air Back in Aerodactyl!. She works on Sayda Island with Gary and is the younger sister of Dora.

She has an astounding relationship with many Pokémon. She has many friends on Sayda Island that will help her when she whistles for them, such as Farfetch'd and Lapras. Along with Gary, she takes care of an Aerodactyl by feeding it.

When the Aerodactyl got out of its containment room in the Lab and flew away, she asked the Lapras to let her know if the Aerodactyl tried to make it off the island, and had the Farfetch'd help her find the Aerodactyl. The Farfetch'd found the Aerodactyl, and she led the rest of the group to where it was hiding.

She assists in preventing Butch and Cassidy from stealing Aerodactyl with the help of her Ivysaur. At the end of the episode, Tracey gives her a sketch of her Ivysaur.
