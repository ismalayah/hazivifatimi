About American Embassy in Sana’a, Republic of Yemen

American Embassy in Sana’a runs an inclusive range of consular services to local, American, and international citizens in Yemen.

The American Embassy in Sana’a supports Yemeni citizens through its consular services, if they:

  • Need general or specific info regarding American economy, culture, sports, education and more
  • Need information regarding requirements and process of getting American Citizenship
  • Specific contacts and information in United States
  • Want to apply and obtain American Visa

Visa and Passport services

American passport and visa from are issued only to citizens whose main residence is in United States and Yemen.

The process of issuing American Visa and Passport of United States can take few weeks.

*Please contact with American Embassy in Sana’a if you have any question as regards of visa and passport matters.

American representations in Yemen

American Embassy in Sana’a is the only American diplomatic mission in Yemen.

    Yemeni representations in United States

    Country of Yemen has 3 other representations in United States:

    • Yemeni Consulate in San Francisco
    • Yemeni Consulate in New York
    • Yemeni Embassy in Washington DC

    American representations worldwide

    American Embassy In Sana’a represents one of 257 consular and diplomatic representations of United States all over the world.

    Foreign representations in Yemen

    American Embassy In Sana’a represents one of 42 foreign consular and diplomatic representations from around the world in Yemen.
