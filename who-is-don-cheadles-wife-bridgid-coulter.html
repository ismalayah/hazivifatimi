Who is Don Cheadles wife Bridgid Coulter?

Publish date: 2024-04-25

Bridgid Coulter is an actress and the wife of Don Cheadle, an American actor. The couple got married in early 2020 after being together for over 28 years. They have two children together.

Don Cheadle shared why he and his decided to get married in 2020, 28 years after they first started dating. “Our accountant proposed to both of us and he showed us the numbers, and we said, ‘Yeah, we should probably do it,’” Cheadle joked to Entertainment Tonight.

He says it did come up over the years, but the two always ended up never really seeing the point. “It came up a lot of times,” Cheadle said, adding, “But every time, we were like, ‘But we’re already together?’

Don Cheadle was with Coulter for the interview, who joked “You’ve got to make sure. I wasn’t sure. Now, I think I’m almost sure.” Cheadle added, “Wait 28 years before you get married. Do not rush into things. Be very careful!”

Bridgid Coulter was born in Alameda County, California, USA on August 2, 1968. Bridgid is an actress who has appeared alongside Don on House of Lies, Black Monday, and in 1997’s Rosewood.
