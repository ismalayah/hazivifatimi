The young star moved to Los Angeles at the age of 19 years to pursue her career in social media. Only two months before stepping into her teen, Kenndy started her YouTube channel called “FabulousInMaking”.

The young star’s confident personality and southern charm made her an instant hit with her fans and made her a teen sensation. Kennedy’s interest in beauty and makeup led her to post makeup tutorials. These tutorials gave lessons on applying makeup in the correct manner.

Whether it is highlighting the eyes or going bold with the lips or simply play safe with a palette of basic yet nude colors, “FabulousInMaking” shows how to do it. In addition to this, there are also some stylish hairdos tutorials that help accentuate the look further.

Over the years, Kennedy’s channel has helped her become one of the most beautiful influencers on YouTube. Her vivacious self and perfect beauty helped her achieve a position in the top 12 NYX Face Awards. As an entrepreneur, Kennedy is also an owner of the KCK (@kckcollection).
