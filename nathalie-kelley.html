Dynasty Actress, Nathalie Kelley Has Separated From Two Years Of Husband, Jordy Burrows in 2020

Publish date: 2024-06-09

Nathalie Kelley is a Peruvian–Australian actress who is best known for starring as Cristal Carrington in Dynasty. Also, she was the cast of the action films The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift, and Furious 7, playing the role of Neela.

More to that, she has played recurring roles as Dani Alvarez in Body of Proof from 2011 to 2012 and as Sybil in The Vampire Diaries from 2016 to 2017. Here is everything you need to know about the Dynasty star.

Who is Nathalie Kelley Is Married? Her Intimate Wedding

The 36 years old Nathalie Kelley is a married woman. She has tied the knot with her husband, Jordan "Jordy" Burrows, 36, in an outdoor ceremony. They began dating in 2018. 

Officially, the couple pronounced as a husband and wife on 29 April 2018 at Potts Point in Sydney following one month of their engagement. The bride was wearing the loose, bohemian-inspired wedding gown paired with a tiara. Whereas the groom donned the black suit with a tie.

The actress' Dynasty co-star Rafael de la Fuente also attended the nuptials. He shared rings with his soulmate,” Jordy wrote on his Instagram post alongside a slideshow of pics. They went to beautiful New South Wales for their honeymoon.

Before exchanging the vows, The Baker and the Beauty Star has announced her engagement with Burrows in April 2018 through her Instagram post. Sadly Nathalie and her spouse of two years, Jordan has announced their seperation in 2020. The divorce is yet to settle.

Kelley's Past Love Affairs

Australian actress, Nathalie who boasts 1.6m followers on her Instagram, has been in several relationships in the past. At first, she was hooked up with the Jonathan Hartlen-Matthews from 2004 to 2005.

On 11th October 2007, the Body of Proof star started dating in Dallas Austin, 50, a musician, songwriter, record and film producer. However, they called it quits in March 2013 after six years of a long term love relationship. 

Then Kelley met actor, Zach Roerig, 36, on the set of The Vampire Diaries in October 2016, where the pair became closer. They made their relationship public in November 2016 through their social media. However, they have broken up in 2017 after one year of courtship.

Kelley made her film debut as Neela in the 2006 film The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift, which grossed $159 million in total.  Her other hit film Furious 7 has become the third highest-grossing film of 2015. It grossed $1.5 billion worldwide on a budget of $190–250 million.

Her other films are Take Me Home Tonight, Urban Explorer, and In Like Flynn. On the other hand, she made her TV show debut in the pilot episode of the tv series Mermaid as Nikki in 2005. Then she went to play many roles in Lone Star, CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, Unreal, Mistresses, The Vampire Diaries and Body of Proof alongside Mary Mouser.

In 2017, Nathalie portrayed in the CW television series Dynasty as Cristal Carrington, playing with Brent Antonello, Elizabeth Gillies, Sam Underwood, and others. In 2020, she was the main cast member of the drama, The Baker and the Beauty. Besides acting, she has made an appearance in TV commercials named Schick Quattro for Women.

Bio and Early Life

Nathalie Kelley was born on 5th October 1984 in Lima, Peru to an Argentine father and Peruvian mother. At age 3, she moved to Australia from Peru along with her family. 

Kelley holds Peruvian citizenship and is of mixed (Argentine and Peruvian) ethnicity. She attended the North Sydney Girls High School in Crows Nest, Australia. Then she enrolled at the University of New South Wales where she received a degree with majors in Third World Development in social science.
