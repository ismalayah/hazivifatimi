The Chainsmokers, NGHTMRE And Seven Lions To Host SMF After Parties

Publish date: 2024-07-05

Recommended Videos

Sunset Music Festival has built a name for itself as one of the best electronic music festivals around, carving out a niche as the only electronic music event serving the East coast for Memorial Day weekend. The festival has seen significant growth since its inaugural event, with a turn out increase from just 5,000 attendees to an expected 30,000 per day this year. In addition to a stacked lineup featuring top tier talent like Jack Ü, Hardwell and Borgore, the Tampa based two day festival has now revealed some enticing after parties.

Following the first night’s festivities on May 28, Seven Lions and NGHTMRE will be hosting afterparties at The Ritz and The Orpheum, respectively. For the second and final night of SMF, The Chainsmokers will hold an after party at The Ritz, while dubstep mastermind Zomboy will throw down at the Cuban Club.
